    Product Description

    Super Brighter & Unique Appearance Design

    EchoLumen solar LED garden lights like a golf clubs or snake, underground stake design. Solar powered no wiring necessary, easy to install. Great widely used for lighting your driveways, pathways, sidewalks, lawn, garden, patio, porch, path, deck, pool, yard, garden, etc.

    Built-in Light Sensor, Auto On/Off

    EchoLumen solar LED garden lights can automatically detect the brightness changes of the external environment to turn on or off. Auto turn the lights on at dusk, auto turn off at dawn, 365 days never turn OFF, solar light can light up to 8 hours after fully charged.

    Two Lighting Modes Option

    Low Light Mode

    Press switch once, the light will automatically turn on at night and turn off at dawn.

    Montion Sensor High Light Mode

    Press switch twice enters Motion Sensor High Lights Mode. In the daytime, it auto-turn off and the solar panel will auto-charge. At night, it auto-go into standby mode. When person enters the PIR sensing distance and range (0-26 feet with a 120 degree motion angle), it will auto turn on the light mode and lasts for 30 seconds, then goes into standby state again.

    IP65 Waterproof Resistant To Any Weathers

    EchoLumen solar LED solar torch light outdoor is made of high-quality ABS plastic and durable construction. EchoLumen LED garden light can also withstand all kinds of severe weather conditions, which is water resistant, heat-resistant and frost resistant. It is capable of withstanding rain, snow, storm and other extreme weather conditions.

    Easy Installation

    Just in few minutes, the solar powered lights can be done on assembling. Attach the lamp assembly to joint to connect pole and slide connect pole into spike. Twist head of lamp to the place where it needs to be illuminated.
