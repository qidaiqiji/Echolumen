    Product Description

    Beautiful New Solar Ground Light

    EchoLumen solar garden lights are much more modern fashionable with unique dome design compared to other flat solar ground lights. 8 leds brighter warm light/cold white, effective but not distracting or annoying. Nice for keeping them in your courtyard, gardens, sidewalks, lawns and corridors, etc., to add a beautiful scene for the summer night.

    Upgraded Design With Robust Lampshade

    Part of the body using high quality ABS material with the special process, which makes the product not only exquisite and classy but also more waterproof and durable than similar solar lights,withstands inclement weather and 60KG standing body.

    Leading IP67 Waterproof

    Our Solar ground lights have been enormous technological breakthroughs in waterproof grade. Unique double IP67 waterproof design which put a silicone seal ring around the edge and built-in waterproof switch. Can really withstand all kinds of terrible weather like a rainstorm, blizzard or daily heat in a long time.

    Automatic Turning ON & OFF

    EchoLumen solar ground lights automatically turn on in darkness or at night and turn off in daytime or bright area. Adding fabulous lighting for your courtyard, gardens, sidewalks, patio, pathway and driveway and you will never fall or lost way at night.

    Reliable Long Working Time

    Built-in 800mAh battery can be illuminated for 8-12 hours after 6 hours of full sunlight charging (The charging time depending on the weather). Please position your outdoor solar lights in a location where it can be exposed to maximum sunlight during the day.

    Easy Ground Spiking Or Wall Mounted Installations

    Turn on the switch under the cap before use (Just use the tip of the stake to press it ), Install the bracket on the back of the solar light and then insert it on your lawn or house wall. Super easy to assemble which just need a few minute!
