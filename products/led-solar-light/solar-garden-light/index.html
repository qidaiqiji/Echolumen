    What you need to know about solar garden light

    With LED technology development and material cost reduction, LED light is now becoming the mainstream lighting products and widely used in commercial facility and ordinary households to replace the traditional old fashioned halogen bulbs. As one of the key segments, solar garden light or LED gardeb light is getting more and more popular to decorate private garden, lawn, public park, walk street and cultural square to provide both lighting effect and visual enjoyment.

    EchoLumen, as a professional solar garden light and solar wall light manufacturer with more than 10 years LED lighting industry experience, is concentrating on designing and manufacturing the most practical and cost-effective solar garden light and LED garden light to cater to the growing market demand.

    What more important is that EchoLumen solar garden light or LED garden light is driven by solar power that is free and easy to get to maintain the constant lighting output during the whole night.

    Here are 4 things you may interested to know about LED garden light or solar garden light. Which help you understand better of the solar garden light and how to choose the solar LED garden light to rich your product fleet.

    Chapter 1 What is LED garden light or solar garden light?

    LED garden light or solar garden light is using LED as its light source and assembled with solar panel at top of the lamp to receive sunlight and transformed it into electric power and restored in the battery placed inside of the lamp body. A light sensor is integrated into the solar panel, in the daytime, the solar panel will charge the battery and the LED garden light or solar garden light will be light up automatically when the ambient light is getting dark. The market demand on LED garden light or solar garden light is massive since such product is cheap in price and low cost to maintain.

    Chapter 2 Types of EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light

    EchoLumen has full ranges of LED garden light or solar garden light products and is capable of supplying the turn key solutions for the lighting projects at private garden, household lawn, public park, commercial street and cultural square etc. We have two main categories of LED garden light or solar garden light with different installations.

    One is LED spotlight or lawn lamp, which you can simply insert its base into ground and expose its solar panel in the sunlight. It is perfect for the installation at lawn, private garden, sides of sidewalks in recreational and residential area for lighting decoration.

    Another is pillar LED solar light, which can be easily mounted to the platform of pillars at residential and recreational area, scenery spots and promenade by the sea or lakes etc.

    Chapter 3 What are features of EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light?

    Elegant design and easy installation solar garden light or LED garden light

    EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light is small in size, exquisitely designed and easy to be installed, especially the LED spot light/lawn lamp which requires no tools for installation.

    Intelligent and practical function LED garden light or solar garden light

    EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light is equipped with one solar panel which integrated one light sensor inside. In the daytime, the solar panel will charge the battery and the LEDs will be light up automatically when it is getting dark.

    Ultra high efficient solar charging and large capacity battery

    EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light is with high quality solar panel, the battery can be fully charged within 5-7 hours in the sunlight and its lighting time can be lasting 3 successive nights (12 hours per night).

    Excellent IP rating and top stability LED garden light or solar garden light

    EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light is with high IP65 rating and top stability to be resistant to any harsh weather conditions.

    No cabling works required LED garden light or solar garden light

    EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light is powered by solar energy, no need any wiring works.

    Chapter 4 Installations of EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light

    The installation of EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light is quite simple. For the LED spot light/lawn lamp, it requires no tools, simply bury its base into ground. As to the installation of pillar LED solar light, you can simply screw its base to the platform of the pillars. No need any extra wiring works.
