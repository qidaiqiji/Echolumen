    Product Description

    Upgraded Solar LED Spotlights

    Brighter 32 LEDs with total 1200 Lumen & wider 150° lighting angle & 90° adjustable bigger solar panel & longer working time with 18650 lithium rechargeable battery. EchoLumen solar landscape lights is much more unique, fashionable and practical compared to other 8 or 12 LED lights in the market.

    High-Efficient Solar Panel

    EchoLumen solar LED garden light outdoor works automatically without electricity as the solar panel absorbs the sun rays and generates power for the light. Our solar panel of monocrystalline silicon converts up to 20% of the sunlight into electricity energy which ensure long lasting standby time.

    3 Brightness Lighting Modes( Always on)

    Dim Light

    Turn on the switch before the first use and press the bottom once, the light will charge during the day and automatically light up Dim Light at night.

    Medium Light

    Make sure the switch is open and press the bottom twice, the light will charge during the day and automatically light up Medium Light at night.

    High Light

    Make sure the switch is open and press the bottom three times, the landscape light will charge during the day and automatically light up High Light at night.

    Waterproof & Heatproof

    Made of high-impact ABS plastic and having been enormous technological breakthroughs in waterproof grade, outdoor solar spotlights have unique waterproof design, it can withstand rain and other extreme weather conditions.

    2 in 1 Function With 2 Installation Options

    Stick into the ground with stakes/Mount on the wall with the screws included. 90° adjustable solar panel offers perfect lighting angle. Optimum for Patio, Porch, Path, Deck, Pool, Yard, Garden, Garage, Driveway, etc.
