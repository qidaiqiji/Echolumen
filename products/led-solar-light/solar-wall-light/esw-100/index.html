    Product Description

    White Cold Lights Illuminate Your Way Home

    270 degrees wide angle illumination angle, unique two light boards design and 100pcs super bright LED beads on our solar lights outdoor, the range of the illumination can up to 30 square meters, offers more lights for your front door, porch, yard, patio, garden, deck, driveway or backyard,etc. Ensure the safety of your family and your neighbors on the way home.

    Auto On/Off By Light Sensor & Motion Sensor

    In the daytime, security lights auto-turn off and the solar panel will auto-charge. At night, the sensor lights auto-go into standby mode. When a person enters the motion detected range (0-26 feet with a 120 degree motion angle), the outdoor lights will auto-turn on by motion sensor technology; when the person leaves the motion detected range, the wall lights will light lasts 30 seconds and turn off then re-enter the standby mode.

    Highly Charging Efficient Solar Panel

    This upgraded wireless solar powered security light adopts a larger mono-crystalline silicon solar panel, achieving a photoelectric conversion rate of up to 20%. The charging time takes only 6-8hours and can maintain a 8-10 hours lighting time. With no electricity consumption and waste gas emission, it simply just utilizes sun power and make it easier for any of us to live an energy-saving and Eco-friendly life.

    Solar Power Energy Saving & Longer Service Life

    EchoLumen solar LED flood lights outdoor motion sensor use solar energy to power the lights. Lighting up your front door while saving your electricity bills! Built in 1800mAh lithium battery, a full charge will take 6 hours of sunlight on sunny days, and the LED solar flood lights can be used for 6-8 hours after fully charged. Service life can approach more than 5000 hours, much longer than other outdoor solar lights.

    IP65 Commercial Grade Waterproof & Heatproof

    EchoLumen solar LED wall light outdoor is IP65 waterproof and heatproof, meets daily waterproofing requirements, so can work normally in extreme climates. No worries about the leakage on rainy days and the explosion on hot days. Suitable for your all season use.

    Extremely Easy Installation

    Simply find a place with sufficient sunshine and fix the light with supplied screws onto it. Then active the light with key pin. The ideal installing height is around 6.5ft(2meters).
