    268 LEDs Super Bright LED Solar Light

    EchoLumen solar LED wall lights outdoor equipped with 268 super bright LED beads, which provides excellent illumination of up to 1800 lumens.     This upgraded wireless solar powered security light adopts a larger mono-crystalline silicon solar panel, achieving a photoelectric conversion rate of up to 20.5%. With no electricity consumption and waste gas emission, it simply just utilizes sun power and make it easier for any of us to live an energy-saving and Eco-friendly life.

    EchoLumen solar LED wall light outdoor is ade of durable ABS material. You don't need to worry about using it in the rain. EchoLumen Solar motion sensor light can also withstand all kinds of severe weather conditions, which is water resistant, heat-resistant and frost resistant.     Motion Sensor Is More Sensitive

    EchoLumen solar LED wall lights are equipped with the more sensitive motion sensor than others. Which can easily prevent thieves from entering your home at night and stealing your property. The built-in PIR motion sensor of our solar LED wall light can detect up to 120 degree with a longer sensor length of 26 feet(about 8m) thus solar sensor lights provides a broader range of lighting.

    Two Motion Sensors, Three Lighting Modes

    1. Constant Dim Mode

    Auto turn on Dim light when in darkness or at night. (No need motion detection)

    2. Dim Light Sensor Mode

    Turn on dim light in darkness or at night when no motion detected. Turn into bright light for 15 seconds when motion detected, then turns into dim light.

    3. Motion Sensor Mode

    Auto turn on bright light in darkness or at night when motion is detected and last about 15 seconds, then turns off auto.
