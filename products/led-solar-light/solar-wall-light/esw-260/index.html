    LED: 0.2W/SMD2835*260pcs

    Li-ion Battery: 2200mAh 3.7V

    Solar Panel: Polysilicon with light sensor 5.5V 1.5W

    Install Height: 3M-4M

    IP Rating: IP65 waterproof

    Solar Charging Time: 7-8 hours by bright sunlight

    Lighting Output Time: 12 hours per night

    Induction Type: Human body infrared sensor

    Product Description

    260 LED Ultra Bright Solar Light Outdoor

    Four side luminescence design makes it a wonderful solar light outdoor which covers a lighting range of more than 300° . Equipped with 260 high-quality and ultra bright LEDs, this solar light will simply light up your way through the darkness.

    Two Motion Sensors, Three Lighting Modes

    EchoLumen newest motion sensor solar powered LED wall light has two sensors, making it more sensitive than others. Three lighting modes for your choice. Constant on Mode(50% brightness during the darkness). Dim Mode(only turns on at 50% brightness when it's dark and 100% brightness when motion detected). Motion Sensor Mode (turns on at 100% brightness if darkness and motion detected the same time).

    Highly Charging Efficient Solar Panel

    This upgraded wireless solar powered security light adopts a larger mono-crystalline silicon solar panel, achieving a photoelectric conversion rate of up to 17% . With no electricity consumption and waste gas emission, it simply just utilizes sun power and make it easier for any of us to live an energy-saving and Eco-friendly life.

    IP65 Waterproof And High Stability

    EchoLumen led solar wall lights is made of high quality ABS material and adopts IP65 waterproof technology for rainy weather. Its high stability enables it a long working hours (more than 50,000 hours). Great workmanship and high quality material keep your house light up every night.

    At Least 8 Hours Charging Time For The First Use

    If it's rainy day or the cloudy day for several days, that may cause the built-in battery power runs out but cannot charge well and then cannot light up at night. So please charge it under sunshine at least 8 hours for the first use.

    Easy Ways To Install

    EchoLumen solar LED wall light needs no complicated cables, you can use the provided screws or 3M double-sided tape to mount it easily. Please kindly note the light will be installed at the plces where it can receive enough sunlight. The recommended installation height would be 2-3m. Perfect for using as a security night light garden, yard, hallway, path, stairs, fence, garage door.

    Remarks

    This product utilizes solar energy. Please choose a location that receives adequate sunshine to optimize its solar charge. The greater the charge received from sunlight, the longer the illumination time. An amounting height of 2 to 2.5 meters is recommended.

    It should be kept away from heat or housed hold garbage and disposed of at the local battery disposal center.
