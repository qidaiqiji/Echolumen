    Chapter 1 What is LED wall light or solar wall light?

    LED wall light or solar wall light is one typical segment of LED lights, which using LEDs as light source and mounted to the wall to provide lighting for the passer-bys. There are 2 main categories of LED wall light or solar wall light, one type is for indoor lighting, which normally is wired and powered by electricity. Another type is outdoor LED lights or outdoor solar lights, which is powered by solar energy and has to be installed outdoor on the wall where the sunlight is not blocked out.

    The main differences for them are the source of power and if need wiring or not. At present, the demand on outdoor solar lights is getting bigger and bigger since one small sized solar panel can supply sufficient power for its lighting output and requires no any wiring/cabling works.
