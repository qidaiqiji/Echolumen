    Product Description

    6000 Lumens Brightness, More Energy-Saving

    EchoLumen LED garage lights use a more advanced motherboard and the high quality LED light bulbs. High-brightness and energy-saving LED bulbs not only provide enough brightness but also reduce power consumption. The white light of 6000K-6500K color temperature offers you the best lighting experience.

    90°Adjustable Design

    With 3 adjustable aluminum LED panels, each LED panel of the garage light can be adjusted from 0°to 90°according to your needs, and you can DIY at different angles. The coverage of light can reach 360°.

    50000 Hours Of Service Life

    The LED garage light made of die-cast aluminum which is high temperature resistant and corrosion resistant. Hollow structure design can accelerate heat dissipation for maximum performance and 50,000 hours of service life.

    Easy To Install, Widely Used

    There is the same screw interface as the LED bulb. You just need to align the E26 screw and rotate it right without any tool. This LED garage light is not only suitable for garage, but also for corridor, workshop, barn, basement, office, exhibition area, stadium, etc.
