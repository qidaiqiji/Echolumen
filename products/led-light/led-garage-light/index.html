    Chapter 1 What is LED garage light?

    LED garage light is one typical segment of LED lights, which using high lumen LEDs as light source to provide sufficient bright lighting for garage, barn, warehouse, basement, workshop, parking lot, supermarket and stadium etc. Due to its excellent color rendering index (Ra reaches to 80) and deformable LED panels, LED garage light are the best lighting solutions for the places where require high illumination and cool white light. EchoLumen LED garage light works ideally with E26/E27 base, the installation is extremely easy, no need any extra cabling or wiring, which is a perfect replacement to your light bulbs and bring you a remarkable lighting experience.

    Wide choices LED garage light
