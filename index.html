    ABOUT ECHOLUMEN

    Your trusted LED lighting solution provider and value-added partner

In order to cater to the precise expectations of our customers, we have divided our products into four ranges: LED car light, LED solar light and LED light (LED garage light). All our products adopt high standard LEDs as light source ensuring its top stability and supreme service time. Among them, the solar garden light and solar wall light are driven by solar power and featured high IP65 to resist any weather conditions. For each product range, EchoLumen has a series of designs for our customers to choose and we are willing and capable of taking OEM/ODM request from customers.

    EchoLumen team are industry veterans with more than 10 years experience of lighting design and production management. In addition, we are cooperating with reputable analytics companies to capture the market trend and provide data support for our product managers and sophisticated product designers to create more innovative and novel products to maintain our competitiveness and offers more business possibilities for our customers.

    As a trusted LED solar light and LED car light manufacturer, our entire LED products manufacturing process takes place under strict supervision of our production engineers. EchoLumen constantly improve the quality performance of our products by adopting new technology and materials to squeeze production costs and help to grow our customers' business.

    what we do

    Wide ranges of LED car light and LED solar light products catering to customers' forwarding needs

    LED Garden Light

    LED Garden Light

    EchoLumen has a wide range of LED car light which including LED headlight bulb (LED car headlight and LED truck headlight), LED off road light, car interior light and atmosphere light for car, SUV and truck etc. Adopting strictly selected high lumen LED chips and delicate designs, EchoLumen's LED car light is featured with high brightness, concentrated beam light and excellent waterproof performance IP68, which ensuring you a best ever driving/visual experience at night and help to reduce the risk of accidents while driving at dark night. EchoLumen LED car light meets all types of sockets like H1, H4, H8, 9005, 9006 etc, basically works for all car brands.

    Solar Garden Light

    Solar Garden Light

    EchoLumen solar garden light or LED garden light is powered by solar energy to maintain the sustainable running of the LED solar light when it is dark. We supply two types of solar LED garden light, one is LED spot light/lawn lamp, which you can simply insert its base into the ground and expose its solar panel in the sun. Another is pillar LED solar light, which can be placed on top of pillars to decorate the garden, park, walk street and cultural square etc. EchoLumen LED garden light or solar garden light not only meets its basic lighting function, what more important is they can beautify the dark night and gives you a wonderful visual enjoyment.

    Solar Wall Light

    Solar Wall Light

    EchoLumen solar wall light or LED wall light is featured with high lumen LEDs for high brightness output and excellent durability and stability, high profile housing and high IP rating IP65 make it working perfectly at any harsh weather conditions. The solar wall light or LED wall light is driven by solar power which is free and easy to get, extremely simple installation and maintenance maintain its operating cost at a very low level. EchoLumen offers numerous designs/appearance of solar wall light or LED wall light for different lighting conditions, the powerful solar panel and high quality battery provide sufficient light supply for a whole night. All these features can meet all your expectations on function, quality and cost effectiveness.

    LED Garage Light

    LED Garage Light

    EchoLumen LED garage light is applying high quality LEDs for high lumen brightness output to ensure the excellent lighting performance. This deformable LED garage light can be folded or unfolded to get different lighting angles. EchoLumen LED garage light is designed with different appearances/styles and different power consumption to get the best lighting effect at different suspension height. The pure white with 6000-6500K color temperature is capable of supplying sufficient light for the garage, workshop, barn, warehouse, supermarket and stadium etc. In addition, the high profile housing aluminum material is working perfect to conduct the heat generated by the LEDs and make sure the best working conditions of the LED garage light.
